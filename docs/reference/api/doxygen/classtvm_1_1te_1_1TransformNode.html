◆ original_variables

Array<IterVar> tvm::te::TransformNode::original_variables

The loop variables that were replaced by the transformation.

Prior to applying a layout transformation, these represent the loops to iterate over a tensor as it is being computed, following a row-major traversal of the tensor's original shape in the compute definition.

◆ transformed_variables

Array<IterVar> tvm::te::TransformNode::transformed_variables

The variables generated by the transformation.

After to applying a layout transformation, these represent the loops to iterate over a tensor as it is being computed, following a row-major traversal of the transformed shape of the tensor.
